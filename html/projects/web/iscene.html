iscene

iscene is a collaboration platform for education sector.

iscene is an international, social and collaborative exchange network for education. The goal of iscene was to help students in different parts of the world collaborate, communicate and work with each other easily. After being selected for a Phase I funding grant from NSF and SBIR, iscene is currently ongoing developement for the next phase.
